Design System
This document defines the visual style for the frontend including colors, typography, and spacing to maintain consistency across the application.

Colors
Primary: Blue (#3B82F6)
Success: Green (#22C55E)
Warning: Orange (#F59E0B)
Danger: Red (#EF4444)
Background: White (#FFFFFF)
Text: Dark Gray (#111827)

Typography
Font: Sans-serif (clean and modern)
Heading: Large, Bold
Subheading: Medium, Semi-bold
Body: Normal
Small: Smaller, light

Spacing
Base unit: 8px
Scale: 8 / 16 / 24 / 32 / 40
